**Pathfinder AI** is an AI‑powered career guidance platform that helps students, job‑seekers, and career changers discover personalized career paths. Users answer three simple questions about their skills, education, and goals – then receive a detailed, actionable recommendation generated by Groq’s Llama 3.1 model. The app is multilingual (English, French, Spanish) and includes a narrated video introduction.

Built for the **IBM Global AI Builders Challenge 2026** by **Gesner Deslandes**, Engineer‑in‑Chief at **GlobalInternet.py**.

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **User opens the AI Career Coach tab.**
2. **Fills in three fields:**
   - Top skills or interests (e.g., programming, healthcare, teaching)
   - Education or experience level (e.g., high school, university, self‑taught)
   - Career goals (e.g., find a stable job, start a business, get a certification)
3. **Clicks "Get AI Career Advice".**
4. **The app sends the input to Groq’s Llama 3.1 model** (via secure API).
5. **The AI returns a personalized 3‑5 sentence recommendation** including specific job titles, skills to develop, and next steps.
6. **User can change the language** in the sidebar and re‑run the query to get advice in French or Spanish.
